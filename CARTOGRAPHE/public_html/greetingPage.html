Welcome to Cartograph-E

A Digital Map of Collective Emotional Life

How did you feel today? What about yesterday? In general?
Did you stop to think about what you felt? Did you even stop to feel it?
Do you ever take the time to find out what those around you are feeling? What about those far from you?
How do you feel about the prospect of feeling-together?

Cartograph-E is an effort to map out our states of emotion. To keep them, to make them viewable, to put them in a necessary context of collectivity.
